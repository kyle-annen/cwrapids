A Sport for Everyone, For Life
The sport of Track & Field has a long and storied past. The words above are the motto of USATF, and we believe in this whole heartedly.
Everyone Is a Coach, Everyone is a Student
While there are some general truths in Track & Field, and in each event, there is something we can all learn from each other. Be open to new things, and be open to something of value coming from each member.
Hard-Work Beats Talent, When Talent Doesn't Work Hard
Track & Field is one of the unique sports where if you work hard enough, everyone can find success. A large portion of our coaches grew up in the Clearwater Valley and competed at a high level in the NCAA, NJCAA, and the NAIA.

None of us were exceptionally talented or the optimum body shape. We worked hard for hours a day, many days a week, for years. Success does not come easy, but the hardwork will repay you many times over in character, achievement and love for a sport which lasts a life-time.
Track & Field is Fun!
For the coaches, Track & Field is just about our favorite thing in the world. Sometimes we get a bit over excited. You can let us know if we need to calm down a bit!
